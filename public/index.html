Format

Focus areas

PDPS connects policy, technical, social, and methodological work. Talks are short with an emphasis on discussion.

Policy and law

Regulation, governance frameworks, enforcement, and legal design.

Technical systems

Privacy-by-design, accountability, and risk assessment methods.

Society and culture

Surveillance practices, social impacts, and lived experience.

Methods and evidence

Empirical studies and cross-disciplinary research tools.

Format & participation

Format

Two thematic blocks per edition, short talks, and generous discussion time.

Submission & review

Calls for submissions are managed in OpenReview, with deadlines announced per edition.

Proceedings

Accepted papers are planned for open-access CEUR-WS proceedings using the CEURART template.

Venue & access

The 2026 workshop is planned in Berlin. Hybrid access details follow the final programme.
